Commercial questions, answered

Do you work after hours so we don't have to close?

Yes. We routinely schedule evenings, weekends, and overnight work so your operation never stops. Tell us your busy hours and we'll plan around them.

Can you provide a Certificate of Insurance?

Yes, on request. We carry full general liability and workers' comp and can send a COI naming your property or business as additional insured.

Will you work as a sub for our GC?

Absolutely. We work as a subcontractor on tenant improvements, buildouts, and remodels. We hit our scope, our schedule, and our number.

Do you handle multiple properties?

Yes. Property managers love having one electrician for every site — same crew, same standards, same direct line. We can set up a preferred-vendor arrangement.

How fast can you get out for an emergency?

Lost power, sparking gear, or a code violation that's holding up your business? Call or text Joe directly at (719) 437-7802 and we'll prioritize you.

Will you pull permits and handle inspections?

Yes. Every job that needs a permit gets one. We coordinate with the local inspector and deliver the signed paperwork for your records.

What size jobs do you take?

Anything from a single fixture replacement to a full small-commercial buildout. If it's outside our wheelhouse, we'll tell you that too.
