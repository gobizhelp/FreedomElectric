Panel upgrade questions, answered

How much does a panel upgrade cost in Colorado Springs?

It depends on size (100A, 200A, 400A), location, and whether the service entrance needs work. We give you a flat-rate quote in writing during the free estimate — no surprise add-ons later.

How long does it take?

Most residential panel upgrades are a one-day job. You'll be without power for a few hours while we coordinate with the utility. We schedule it for a time that works for you.

Do you pull the permit and handle the inspection?

Yes. We pull the permit, schedule the inspection, and coordinate with the utility for the meter swap. You don't have to chase anything down.

Why are Federal Pacific and Zinsco panels a problem?

Both have well-documented failure rates — breakers that don't trip when they should. Insurance companies have started requiring them to be replaced. Replacing them is the single best safety upgrade you can make.

Will my insurance company want to know?

Most do, in a good way. Many carriers offer better rates for homes with modern 200A panels and have started flagging older panels. We can give you the documentation you need.

Can you do the panel AND the EV charger / hot tub at the same time?

Yes — and bundling them saves money. We size the new panel to handle everything you're planning, then run the new circuits as part of the same job.

What if I'm not sure I even need an upgrade?

That's what the free estimate is for. Joe will look at your panel, your load, and what you're planning, and give you a straight answer. Sometimes the answer is "you're fine for now." We'll tell you that too.
