What's wired in

Every landing page below is built mobile-first, accessibility-tested, SEO-friendly, and instrumented with the tracking events listed below.

Conversion-focused layout

Dual hero CTA (call + text), sticky mobile contact bar, multiple lead forms, exit-intent capture.

Life Force 8 copywriting

Safety, family protection, peace of mind, social proof, and the desire to make the right decision — woven into every headline and section.

Click-to-call & text tracking

Every phone and SMS link fires a tracked event with location context, plus pushes to GA4, GTM, and Meta Pixel.

Full event tracking

Page view, scroll depth (25/50/75/100%), time on page (10/30/60/120/240s), button clicks, form starts, field focus, submissions, FAQ opens, exit intent.

UTM & ad-click capture

UTM source/medium/campaign/term/content, gclid, fbclid, msclkid — captured on first touch and attached to every lead.

Schema markup

Local Electrician schema on every page so Google can show your license, hours, area served, and rating.

Heatmap support

Drop in your Microsoft Clarity or Hotjar ID and recordings + click maps start flowing immediately.

Webhook lead delivery

Pipe leads to Formspree, Zapier, Make, n8n, Slack, your CRM — anywhere that accepts a JSON POST.

Admin editing

Edit headlines, subheadlines, FAQs, CTAs, testimonials, service areas, and toggle entire sections on/off without touching code.
